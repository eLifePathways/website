About Pathways

Why eLife Pathways exists.

Supported by Wellcome, Pathways is a technology initiative from eLife that partners with communities and organisations to develop new open-source tools and infrastructure that enable innovative and more inclusive peer-review and publishing models.

About eLife Pathways

Who eLife Pathways is for.

We collaborate with like-minded organisations and communities of researchers that are looking for technology support or expertise to deliver new technologies that benefit scholarly communication.

How it works

What eLife Pathways does.

With our partners, we build open-source, modular tools and infrastructure for better end-to-end publishing and peer-review workflows. They're free to use, adapt and extend by anyone, and we can partner to explore or pilot new publishing models.
