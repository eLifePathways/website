pageHeading: Interoperable infrastructure for the open science ecosystem
intro:
  - >-
    The greatest untapped potential in open infrastructure lies in its
    underlying “plumbing”. By prioritising seamless integration and
    interoperability we transform existing open-source tools into
    high-performance systems for the global community. Our development of
    <a href="https://sciety.org/">Sciety</a> and
    <a href="https://kotahi.community/">Kotahi</a> establishes an adaptable
    foundation for scholarly communication, allowing us to build new
    community-led prototypes that bridge existing gaps and unify the open
    science ecosystem.
sections:
  - heading: Open Publishing
    body:
      - >-
        We provide a flexible, end-to-end platform for modernising
        submission, review, and publication. Kotahi is designed for
        flexibility, it allows users to configure custom workflows that meet
        the unique demands of open publishing. By eliminating licensing fees
        and overhead, Kotahi offers a sustainable, adaptable solution built
        specifically for grassroots initiatives and Diamond Open Access
        models.
  - heading: Preprints and early sharing
    body:
      - >-
        We provide an interoperable architecture that transforms original
        manuscripts into high-quality HTML preprints, featuring bespoke
        layouts alongside dynamically generated, production-ready PDFs.
        These systems support flexible screening workflows, integrated task
        management, and AI-driven assistance to provide efficient, scalable
        validation for diverse, early-stage research objects.
  - heading: Review and Curation
    body:
      - >-
        We provide the infrastructure for modern research sharing, including
        preprint review and the publish-review-curate (PRC) model. By
        decoupling evaluation from publishing, our tools give communities
        autonomy over the review process. Kotahi enables integrated
        custom review and evaluation workflows. Sciety aggregates
        review and evaluation across scholarly networks, providing the tools
        to discover and organise all curated objects across the entire
        research ecosystem.
  - heading: Open Research Information
    body:
      - >-
        We specialise in open research information and scholarly metadata.
        Because a thriving open science ecosystem depends on robust
        connections, we enrich and enhance scholarly metadata to create
        seamless links between research outputs, rapidly prototyping new
        ways to showcase open research innovation.
  - heading: "Expertise on open\u2011source software delivery"
    body:
      - >-
        We help navigate the complexities of open-source development with
        technical guidance and community-focused expertise. Whether launching
        a new innovation or adopting an existing tool, we work alongside the
        community to create a culture of agility and shared ownership,
        ensuring research technology delivers immediate and lasting value to
        the scholarly community.
